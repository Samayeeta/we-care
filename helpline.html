Suicide Helpline Numbers

It's critical to talk about mental health. It can, however, be difficult for certain people and can elicit strong emotional responses. WeCare encourages you to seek assistance from your local health care facility, a local community organisation that specialises in mental health, or a national helpline (if one is available). There's nothing to lose by giving it a try. Calling a suicide hotline is an excellent way to express yourself while still receiving good advice about how to cope with your issues. This is a great place to start if you want to support yourself or someone else. The following is a comprehensive list of countries and the phone numbers for their related suicide hotlines.
