About WeCare

The shortage of mental health care providers and facilities continues to leave millions of cancer patients without access to the care they need. Although telemedicine is able to address many of these shortfalls, particularly for people in rural areas or other regions with little access to care, huge gaps remain. Most terminally ill patients, for example, report having some optimistic feelings (65%), but a small percentage (3%) have recurrent suicidal thoughts, which is a sign of distress. Furthermore, some patients have pre existing psychiatric problems that are worsened by advanced disease, while others experience new anxiety or depression symptoms as their illness progresses. And, at some point, both patients will have to confront the daunting task of death planning. WeCare is a global platform that aims to raise awareness on the usage of biotechnology to cure mental illnesses. We strive to create a safe space for advanced cancer patients and provide free resources to biotech and oncology enthusiasts.
